## **Understanding Left Shift (`<<`)**

**`<<` shifts all bits to the LEFT by specified positions, filling with 0s on the right.**

**Formula:**

## **Bitwise Operators Comparison:**

| Operator | Name | Effect | Example |
|----------|------|--------|---------|
| `<<` | Left Shift | Multiply by 2^n | `5 << 2 = 20` |
| `>>` | Right Shift | Divide by 2^n | `20 >> 2 = 5` |
| `>>>` | Unsigned Right Shift | Divide (fills 0) | `20 >>> 2 = 5` |
| `&` | AND | Bitwise AND | `5 & 3 = 1` |
| `\|` | OR | Bitwise OR | `5 \| 3 = 7` |
| `^` | XOR | Bitwise XOR | `5 ^ 3 = 6` |
